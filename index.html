I'm Dylan Uyttersprot

I'm a 21 year old student living in Belgium. I study Business Engineering (currently in my third bachelor) at the University of Ghent. Besides economics I'm also interested in mathematics, physics, philosophy and so on. But my biggest interests consist mainly of travelling, music and my obsession with the Vosseslag. 

If you want to know more about my ordinary life, feel free to scroll down and see some of the exciting moments I've experienced. One of these moments is the beginning of my new start-up company Un-Ecycle in 2021. 

I've had the privilege to travel already quite a lot. I got to know different people and cultures around the world, some of them became close friends of me. But I'm most grateful for my 3 best friends: Dauchy, Marlot (ex-girlfriend from leek) and Mr. Pauwels.

My most valuable picture

I remember this evening at the Overpoort (Ghent) like it was yesterday. The boys and me were having a couple of drinks when all of a sudden, the earth beneath us started shaking. We all thought it was nothing but we could hear the screaming of several girls (and Jens De Keyzer) in the distant. I couldn't believe my eyes, a mysterious man was approaching our way. All of a sudden, standing right in front of me was international superstar Niels Destadsbader. He politely asked to take a picture with us and the rest is history...
